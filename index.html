Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule an appointment by navigating to the Appointments section and clicking on "Schedule New Appointment" button. Follow the prompts to select a provider, date, and time.
How can I access my medical records?
Your medical records can be accessed in the Records section. You can filter by record type and download or view your records directly from the platform.
How do I request a prescription refill?
To request a prescription refill, go to the Prescriptions section, find the medication you need refilled, and click the "Request Refill" button.
What should I do in a medical emergency?
For medical emergencies, call 911 immediately or go to the nearest emergency room. The University Health Center emergency line is (555) 911-0000.
